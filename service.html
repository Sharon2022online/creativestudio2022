Our Services

We provide 3 main services

Food Donation

Giving is excellent karma, but giving food to the poor is mahadan (generous donation). The world is a beautiful and a better place to live when food is donated to those in need. Give food to those who are less fortunate, and you'll feel peace and tranquilly in your soul as well as a connection to God. No one will go to bed hungry if we all follow this same path.

Blood Donation

Donating Blood is the most priceless gift someone can give to another. Donating blood can save a life, or perhaps multiple lives if the blood's components, red blood cells, platelets, and plasma, can be used separately to treat patients with different illnesses.

Sponser A Child

Today, you can sponsor a child and help a whole neighbourhood. Your contributions will enable more kids to receive an education, giving them the tools they need to speak up, stay in school longer, and put an end to destructive customs like child marriage. Your contribution will provide communities with access to water, healthcare, and emergency protection.
